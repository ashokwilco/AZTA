Our Story

Preserving Heritage,
Building Community

The Arizona Telugu Association is a vibrant community organization dedicated to preserving and promoting Telugu culture, language, and heritage across the great state of Arizona. We are your home away from home.

🎯 Our Mission

Fostering community among Telugu-speaking residents through cultural, educational, and social events for all generations.

🌿 Our Vision

Keeping Telugu language, traditions, and values alive for future generations while promoting unity across all Indian communities in Arizona.

🎭 Festivals

We celebrate Ugadi, Sankranti, Bathukamma, Diwali, and more with vibrant performances and traditional food.

🤝 Community

Supporting charity initiatives and collaborating with other cultural associations to promote diversity and togetherness.
